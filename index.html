Hi 👋🏾

More than pretty pictures.

Most people know me as That Canva Girl. Canva has always been my playground — I've designed 100+ LinkedIn profiles, banners, and carousels for consultants, founders, and creators.

But somewhere between profile #50 and #100, I noticed something: the clients actually getting clients weren't the ones with the prettiest designs. They understood the game — positioning, content, engagement, all of it. So I learned that too (because why not 🤓).

Now I help consultants and service providers get strategically and visually positioned on LinkedIn — #1 in Branding & Positioning on LinkedIn Kenya, #40 worldwide. Not that I'm counting 😌
