Frequently asked questions

The things everyone asks right before they slide into the DMs 😌

01

How long does this whole thing take?

Depends on what you get. A banner takes longer than a carousel or flyer, and bigger profile rebuilds run longer than single assets. Reach out and I'll give you a real timeline. Heads up: if I'm booked out, that adds to it.

02

Do I have to write my own copy?

Depends on what you're after. For carousels and template purchases, you usually send me the text. For LinkedIn profile rebuilds, I handle strategic positioning and hook copy for the visuals. If you'd rather I write more, we can chat about a content add-on.

03

Which Canva plan do I need?

Free Canva works for most layouts. But honestly? Canva Pro unlocks the good stuff: brand kits, premium elements, the whole vibe.

04

Can't I just do this myself?

Sure. You absolutely can. Just like I could rewire my own house. But why spend weeks figuring out what works when I've already spent thousands of hours testing and perfecting this for 100+ brands? Your time is worth more than that. And honestly? So is your reputation.

05

When is the next Canva coaching cohort?

Cohorts run three times a year, and the next one kicks off in June 2026. This isn't just social-media design. It's Canva mastery for building brands that look premium. DM me to get on the waitlist before spots fill up.
