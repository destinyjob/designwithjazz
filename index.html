Hi 👋🏾

More than pretty pictures.

I've loved design for as long as I can remember. I just didn't know it was called "graphic design" back then. These days, most people know me as The Canva Girl. I've designed 100+ LinkedIn profiles, banners, and carousels for consultants, founders, and thought leaders.

But somewhere between profile #50 and #100, I noticed something: the clients actually getting clients weren't the ones with the prettiest designs. They understood the game (positioning, content, engagement, all of it). So I learned that too (because why not 🤓).

Now I help consultants and service providers get strategically and visually positioned on LinkedIn. As a Canva Ambassador, I've spent years testing what actually works on LinkedIn versus what's just noise. #1 in Branding & Positioning on LinkedIn Kenya, #40 worldwide. Not that I'm counting 😌

Here's my thing: you can't leave my work the same way you found it. Whether it's your LinkedIn profile, your graphics, or a single post, I make you feel something. Because in a sea of cookie-cutter content, that's what makes people stop, click, and say, "I need to work with this person."
